Executive Summary

Top performers win by selling outcomes first, not catalog size. The best platforms keep one dominant CTA, show trust early, and structure discovery around roles or goals.

Yanfaa already has a broad catalog and clear affordability message. The biggest opportunity is converting this into role-based pathways with stronger proof placement and a tighter conversion journey.

SEO Landscape

  • High-intent Arabic keywords: دورات اونلاين، منصة تعليمية عربية، مسارات تعلم، دورات معتمدة.
  • Role-based pages outperform generic catalogs in long-tail capture.
  • Yanfaa opportunity: indexable, static content layers for role/outcome pages.

Patterns of the Top 10%

  • Outcome-first hero messaging.
  • One dominant CTA repeated with consistency.
  • Trust signals above the fold.
  • Goal-based navigation over category overload.
  • Mobile-first conversion optimization.

Recommended Direction for Yanfaa

  • Keep “one subscription” position, but pair with role outcomes (e.g., SEO Specialist in X weeks).
  • Introduce guided pathways as primary navigation object.
  • Move proof closer to hero: learner counts, outcomes, instructor credibility.
  • Use cleaner Arabic typography hierarchy and lighter visual rhythm for readability.
