## Patterns of the Top 10%

1. **Outcome-first positioning beats content-first positioning.**
   - Winners sell transformation (job, promotion, practical skill), not just course availability.

2. **Single, repeated primary CTA across the page.**
   - High-performing platforms minimize choice overload and keep one dominant next action.

3. **Proof appears early, not deep in page.**
   - Credibility elements (partners, instructor authority, numbers, testimonials) are introduced near the hero.

4. **Catalog depth is organized around user intent, not platform taxonomy.**
   - “What you want to become” pathways outperform giant undifferentiated course libraries.

5. **Mobile UX is conversion UX.**
   - High performers optimize touch targets, concise copy blocks, and low-friction starts.

## SEO Landscape — Opportunity Summary

### Dominant keyword clusters in this niche
- `دورات اونلاين`
- `تعلم عن بعد`
- `منصة تعليمية عربية`
- `مسارات تعلم`
- `دورات معتمدة`
- Career-specific intent (`UI UX`, `SEO`, `Media Buying`, `Data Analysis`, etc.)

### Gaps Yanfaa can exploit
- Stronger localized long-tail pages by role/outcome (`تعلم [مهارة] للمبتدئين`, `مسار [وظيفة] خلال [مدة]`).
- Comparison pages (`Yanfaa vs ...`) for high-intent users.
- Better indexable static content for key landing pages (current SPA shell limits crawl-visible narrative).
